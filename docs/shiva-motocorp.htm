I need urgent help - how do I get in touch with Land Rover's Roadside Assistance (RSA)?
I need urgent help - how do I get in touch with Land Rover's Roadside Assistance (RSA)?

Our Operations Centre is staffed by experienced staff and it's open 24 hours a day, 365 day a year. The toll-free Roadside Assistance service (RSA) number to call for Land Rover India is is 1800-103-8545. When you call, we'll ask you for:

  • Your name
  • Your location
  • Your registration number or Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)
  • A brief description of the incident
  • A contact telephone number

If your car has been involved in an accident, we'll also ask you to describe the damage and tell us the details of anyone else involved. Do take note that your vehicle would need to be under warranty - other Terms and Conditions may apply.
